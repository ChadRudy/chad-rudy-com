About Chad

Chad Rudy is a CERTIFIED FINANCIAL PLANNER™ professional and Executive Vice President at Retirement Investment Advisors, Inc. With more than 15 years of experience, he provides comprehensive, fee-only financial planning for individuals and families seeking to achieve their long-term goals.


His expertise spans retirement planning, investment management, tax strategies, education funding, and estate planning. Before becoming a financial advisor, Chad held leadership roles at Fortune 100 companies and owned a successful software business, giving him a unique perspective in serving clients.
